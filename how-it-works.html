How the Multi‑Agent System Works

A coordinated workflow where each agent contributes its strengths.

1. Understanding the User’s Request

The Customer Service Agent begins by clarifying the user’s needs, asking questions, and ensuring the system fully understands the goal.

2. Research & Information Gathering

The Research & Analysis Agent collects relevant information, summarizes data, and provides factual context for the task.

3. Strategic Evaluation

The Business Strategy Agent evaluates feasibility, planning, and long‑term considerations to guide the direction of the solution.

4. Financial & Market Review

The Financial Agent analyzes cost, ROI, and market conditions to ensure the solution is grounded in realistic expectations.

5. Specialized Expertise

The Industry‑Specific Agent applies domain knowledge to refine the solution with expert‑level insight.

6. Communication & Presentation

The Sales & Persuasion Agent improves clarity, tone, and messaging to ensure the final output is polished and compelling.

7. Final Synthesis

The Master Strategy Agent (CEO AI) combines all insights into a unified, high‑quality final answer.
